# Sub-processors - TauCAD Limited

*Effective date: December 27, 2025*

This page lists all third-party service providers (sub-processors) that process personal data on behalf of TauCAD Limited in connection with our Service. This list is maintained in accordance with our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy) and applicable data protection laws.

We maintain Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with all sub-processors listed below, as required by GDPR Article 28 and equivalent provisions in other privacy laws.

## Infrastructure and Hosting

| Sub-processor | Purpose | Data Processed | Location |
|---------------|---------|----------------|----------|
| **Fly.io** | API application hosting | All service data, IP addresses, server logs | USA |
| **Netlify** | Frontend hosting and CDN | Static assets, IP addresses, request headers | USA |

## AI and Machine Learning Services

When you use AI-assisted CAD features, your prompts and relevant context may be processed by the following providers. Your data is not retained by these providers for their own training purposes.

| Sub-processor | Purpose | Data Processed | Location |
|---------------|---------|----------------|----------|
| **OpenAI** | AI language models (GPT) | Prompts, CAD context, conversation history | USA |
| **Anthropic** | AI language models (Claude) | Prompts, CAD context, conversation history | USA |
| **Google (Vertex AI)** | AI model services (Gemini) | Prompts, CAD context, conversation history | USA |
| **SambaNova** | AI model services (optional) | Prompts, CAD context when model is selected | USA |
| **Cerebras** | AI model services (optional) | Prompts, CAD context when model is selected | USA |
| **Tavily** | Web search for AI features | Search queries when web search is enabled | USA |
